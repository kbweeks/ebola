Ebola Across The World

In December 2013, the world's deadliest and most widespread Ebola virus outbreak began in Guinea. As the virus continues to claim the lives of thousands of West Africans and affect other continents, the disease has caused a panic and even has become a popular discussion topic. Here, you can learn about the current outbreak, find the latest news updates, view photographs of affected areas and those working to fight Ebola, see up-to-the minute Twitter updates and learn even more.
